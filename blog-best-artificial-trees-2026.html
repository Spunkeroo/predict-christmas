Best Artificial Christmas Trees

Finding the perfect Christmas tree should not require a trip to a tree farm in freezing weather. Modern artificial trees look remarkably realistic with PE (polyethylene) branch tips that mimic real needles. Pre-lit models eliminate the hassle of stringing lights. How to Choose

  • PE vs PVC tips: PE (polyethylene) branches are molded from real trees and look incredibly realistic. PVC branches are cheaper but flatter. Best trees mix both — PE on the outer visible tips, PVC for interior fullness
  • Pre-lit vs unlit: Pre-lit saves hours of setup. Look for LED lights (last 50,000+ hours vs 3,000 for incandescent). Warm white LEDs look most natural
  • Height guide: Measure your ceiling and subtract 12 inches for a tree topper. 7-7.5 ft is ideal for standard 8-9 ft ceilings

Setup Tips

Fluffing matters: Spend 30-45 minutes shaping branches after setup. Start from the bottom, work up, and pull branch tips toward you and slightly upward. A well-fluffed artificial tree is indistinguishable from real at 6 feet.

Storage: Invest in a rolling tree bag (-) to protect your investment. Store in a dry area. Properly stored artificial trees last 15-20 years.

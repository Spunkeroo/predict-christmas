Cheapest Days to Fly

  • Thanksgiving: Fly Tuesday or Wednesday before, return the following Saturday or Sunday (not Sunday of Thanksgiving weekend)
  • Christmas: Fly December 23 or 24 (fewer people fly on Christmas Eve). Return December 27 or January 2+
  • Avoid: December 22 and 26 are the most expensive days of the year to fly

Airport Survival Tips

  • TSA PreCheck (/5 years): Skip the regular security line. Worth every penny during holidays when lines exceed 60 minutes
  • Arrive 3 hours early: Holiday airports are chaos. Build in buffer time for parking, check-in, security, and gate changes
  • Download airline app: Get real-time gate changes, delay notifications, and mobile boarding passes. Airlines notify app users before updating departure boards
  • Pack a charger and snacks: Outlets are scarce during peak travel. Bring a portable battery and protein bars in case of delays

Road Trip Tips

Gas savings: Use GasBuddy app to find cheapest stations along your route. Fill up before entering turnpikes and interstate rest areas where prices are 20-40% higher. Top off before entering rural stretches.

Timing: Leave before 6 AM or after 8 PM to avoid peak traffic. Wednesday before Thanksgiving is the busiest driving day of the year — if possible, leave Tuesday night.
